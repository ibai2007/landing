1. Explications

France has thousands of cities, each with its own identity and traditions. Many of them are known for their famous culinary specialties. For example, Lyon is considered the gastronomic capital of France and is famous for its traditional dishes. Marseille is known for “bouillabaisse”, Toulouse for “cassoulet”, and Nice for “salade niçoise”. Overall, there are many major French culinary specialties, often linked to specific cities and their local ingredients. This rich diversity makes French cuisine unique and renowned all over the world..
